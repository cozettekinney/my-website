My name is Cozette Kinney, and I go by Cozy.

One of my favorite activities is hiking and exploring the outdoors. Growing up in Colorado, I have always been surrounded by nature and mountain hikes. My family spends as much time as possible outside. I try to spend time outside every day, from running, walking my dog in the open space, going on longer hikes, or camping. This year, the weather has been very dry and warm in Colorado, making it perfect hiking weather. When I am home, I went hiking at my local trailhead nearly every day. Pictured below, Chautauqua Park in Boulder, Colorado has lots of super fun hikes through the Flatirons, which are the iconic rock formations in Boulder. I enjoy the difficult hikes up the Flatirons. Scrambling and climbing the Flatiron rocks is always a fun challenge. My friends and I do annual sunrise hikes up to the Royal Arch, which is a natural rock arch that requires strenuous climbs. The views from the top of the Flatirons overlook CU Boulder, the entire front range, and Long's Peak. I'm grateful I grew up in this area and had the chance to fall in love with the mountains from a young age.
